# ADR-NNNN: Short title of the decision

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [ADR-XXXX](./XXXX-slug.md)
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** names or roles

## Context

What problem are we solving? What forces are at play? What would happen if
we did nothing? Keep it to what a reader cold-reading this ADR needs to
understand the decision.

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly. Present tense, imperative voice.
"We use X to do Y." Not "We will consider X."

## Alternatives considered

Each alternative gets a paragraph:

- **Alternative A** — one-line summary. Why it was tempting. Why we didn't
  pick it.
- **Alternative B** — one-line summary. Why it was tempting. Why we didn't
  pick it.

Be honest. If the choice was close, say so. Future contributors deserve to
know which dragons are still lurking.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what we commit to maintaining.
Include the non-obvious downstream effects — the stuff a reader wouldn't
realize from the decision alone.

## References

- Links to upstream specs, issues, PRs, conversations that shaped the
  decision.
